Classifying AI: Capabilities

ANI (Narrow)

Designed for one specific task (e.g., spam filters). This is the most common AI today.

AGI (General)

Theoretical AI that rivals human intelligence across all areas.

ASI (Super)

Hypothetical AI that exceeds human intelligence (linked to "Singularity").

Functionality: AI Expert Systems

Role: Simulates human expertise in a specific area, like travel planning.

Mechanism: Combines a Knowledge Base (facts) with an Inference Engine (AI brain).

Functionality: Predictive AI

Purpose: Uses historical data to forecast the future (e.g., flight delays, weather)
